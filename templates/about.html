About Us

Grocelicious is a web scraping app that provides you with a hassle-free way to compare prices between two leading online grocery stores, Dunzo and Blinkit. Our mission is to help you save time and money on your grocery shopping by making it easy to find the best deals.

How it Works

Using Grocelicious is simple. Just enter the name of the product you are looking for, and we will provide you with a list of prices from both Dunzo and Blinkit. You can then choose the store that offers the best deal and save money on your grocery shopping.

Features

  • Compare prices between two leading online grocery stores, Dunzo and Blinkit
  • Save time and money on your grocery shopping
  • Hassle-free and easy to use
  • No need to visit multiple stores or websites to find the best deal
  • Regularly updated prices to ensure accuracy
